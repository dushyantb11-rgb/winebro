## Section 5 — Investor / advisor one-pager

### What is it
A pocket sommelier app for the Indian market: scan a bottle, ask what pairs with biryani, log what you drank, see what your friends are pouring. Free to use, monetised via affiliate revenue and (later) a paid AI-sommelier tier.

### The market gap
- India alcobev market: **₹2.5L Cr ($30B+) by 2027**, growing 7% YoY
- Wine alone: **₹3,500 Cr today, projected ₹14,000 Cr by 2030** (4× in 6 years)
- **Zero existing apps** built for the Indian palate. Vivino works for Tuscany; Indian users get blank stares from "what wine pairs with rajma chawal?"
- Indian consumers under-buy wine because **they don't know what to drink with their food**. WineBro removes that friction.

### Proprietary data assets (10 D-series — already collecting from day 1)
| Asset | What | Why it's defensible |
|---|---|---|
| D1 | Indian-dish ↔ bottle pairing matrix | Largest curated dataset on the subcontinent; competitors would need 18 months to replicate |
| D3 | Indian-context aroma vocabulary (Aam, Jamun, Imli) | Cultural moat — non-replicable by Western apps |
| D5 | Cross-category preference graph | "Red wine drinkers who also drink craft beer" — brand B2B gold |
| D6 | Pairing success rate (Yes/Maybe/No feedback) | Self-improving engine; every user makes the next user's recommendation better |
| D7 | Restocking behaviour | Predicts repeat purchase intent — affiliate conversion lift |
| D10 | Occasion-context patterns | "X% of Sula Sauvignon happens at restaurants vs home" — partnership intel |

### Tech & infra
- 35 PRs merged in 7 production sprints; 99/99 unit tests; 11 Cloud Functions live
- Flutter (iOS + Android, single codebase) + Firebase (Firestore asia-south1 + FCM + Storage + Cloud Functions)
- Pairing engine v1.1 with Bayesian-shrunk community feedback consumption already shipping
- Privacy: SHA-256 phone-hash friend discovery, owner-only journal reads, age-gated signup

### Revenue model (3 horizons)
1. **Now (M1-M6)** — affiliate commerce. Every "Buy" tap → Flipkart / BigBasket / Amazon partner link. ~₹50-150 per conversion.
2. **M6-M12** — premium tier (Razorpay): AI sommelier chat, advanced pairing engine access, no affiliate banners. ₹199/month or ₹1,499/year.
3. **M12+** — B2B brand dashboard. Sula, Grover, Amrut, Bira pay for cohort reports + featured surfacing. ₹50K-2L/quarter per partner.

### Ask
*(Customise this section by audience)*
- For pre-seed angels: **₹50L for 18 months runway** to 50K MAU + first paid tier launch
- For brand BD partners: **soft cross-promo + tasting team time**, no money
- For advisors: **0.25-0.50% equity** for monthly strategic touchpoints
